Our Mission

"Helping companies —

whether they're going public or newcomers."

HTY's mission statement is broad in purpose, proving that not all mission statements have to be tailored specifically to a company's business. This inspiring statement focuses on their audience, creating a direct connection with their readers, which isn't surprising given their creative and personal marketing history. HTY markets itself as a group of service-oriented people committed to "making all of our clients' companies better", so this mission statement is a good reflection of their brand and company personality.

HTY's history

The three partners of HTY were classmates in college. By chance, they wanted to complete the task of making a website together, and what they thought of was to make an IT website. This is also the reason why they started working as a freelancer together and brought various services step by step to today. They also began to expand their service scope from outside Malaysia to the Asia region step by step.

Those who are interested can come and check out the latest technological services provided by HTY and some of their thoughts on these technologies.
