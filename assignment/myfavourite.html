My Favourite

There are many things I am interested in, but the one that attracts me
the most is sports. This is because physical activity can improve
cardiovascular health, reduce disease risks and help manage weight.

Among many sports, badminton, basketball and football attract me the
most. Because there are player in all three sports that I admire and like.

Lee Zii Jia

(Malaysian Badminton Player)

In badminton, my favourite player is Lee Zii Jia from Malaysia. His fierce style of play, violent smashing and solid defense make his game extremely enjoyable. Fans can feast their eyes whenever he plays. And as a Malaysian, he also carries the hope of Malaysia winning the Olympic gold medal for the first time.

Lionel Messi

(Argentina Football Player)

In football, my favourite player is Lionel Messi from PSG / Argentina. What attracts me to Messi lies in his humble character and excellent ball skills. And he is also the most agile and practical player I have seen dribbling. His excellent ball-handling ability allowing him to turn his waist as fast as he can to deceive opponents trying to take the ball away from him.
